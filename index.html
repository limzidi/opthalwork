Dr. Evelyn Tai

Dr Evelyn Tai is a Fellow of the Royal College of Ophthalmologists and the International Council of Ophthalmology. As the postgraduate coordinator of her university’s Masters of Medicine in Ophthalmology program, she is active in teaching, training and supervision. She is also a keen researcher, holding several grants and publishing more than 30 articles to date. She is currently exploring opportunities to further her fellowship training.
